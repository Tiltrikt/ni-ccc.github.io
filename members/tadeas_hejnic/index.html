Ideas

I have a few project ideas I would like to start working on:

App for Physiotherapists and Trainers

This idea originated during a conversation with my physiotherapist. The goal is to easily demonstrate how muscles work during different types of movement. Currently, there are many animations available, but nothing interactive AFAIK.

In short:

  • Develop a 3D "viewer" application.
  • Allow users to interactively move parts of the human body.
  • Visualize muscle activity using a gradient from green (maximum extension) to red (maximum tension).

Infographics Generator for Sports Clubs

This project idea comes from the poor visual presentation of many sports clubs on social media, especially in handball. The goal is to provide a user-friendly tool, possibly as a Canva plug-in, since most admins already use it.

In short:

  • Connect variable content (scores, team logos, text fields) via league APIs or similar data sources.
  • Offer predefined, professional-looking templates that can be easily customized.
